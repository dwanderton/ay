How to get here

General Notes

If this is your first time in the USA or in Vermont you might be suprised to hear public transportation is incredibly sparse and to our venues non-existent. Once you arrive by plane, or if you live locally, driving is the only viable option. If you feel uncomfortable driving in the USA or locally within the New England area please reach out to us and we will try to help you to make arrangements to car share or find an alternative solution. The driving style in Boston and Montreal is similar to any major metropolitan area - however once you are outside of the city driving becomes much more of a pleasure and other drivers become more considerate.

Also unless you are staying close to the barn it is worth getting in contact with a local taxi firm, listed below, or having a designated driver.

Looking for suggstions on where to stay? We have some in our FAQs at the bottom of our homepage. Here are a few resources to find a great place to stay in Waitsfield, Vermont: Airbnb.com and HomeAway.com. Skinner Barn also recommends using the Mad River Valley Chamber of Commerce to find lodging information and dining/activities/services information.

From Boston

The drive from Boston is just under 200 miles and takes between 3 to 3 and a half hours depending on the trafic level. The Google Maps directions can be found by clicking here. The journey is quite beautiful once you enter New Hampshire and involves a drive through the incredible wooded areas of southern Vermont State.

From Montreal

The drive from Montreal is just over 150 miles and takes between 3 to 3 and a half hours depending on the trafic level - more or less the same from Boston. The Google Maps directions can be found by clicking here. The journey is also spectacular and it doesn't take any longer to route through the islands inbetween New York State and Vermont. Set your GPS to go via Alburg, North Hero or Grand Isle and don't miss the amazing views of Lake Champlain.

From Burlington/Montpelier Vermont

Once you are heading south on I-89 from Burlington or north on I-89 from Montpelier it’s as simple as taking exit 10! Take VT-100 South towards Waterbury (away from Ben and Jerrys!) for about 500ft then take a left at the roundabout and the church is 500ft on your right.

From West Coast USA / United Kingdom

For those traveling from the West Coast of the USA or the UK we reccomend flying into Boston Logan International Airport. Although Boston is slightly further away than Montreal the transit time to the venue is more or less the same. By arriving in Boston you also give us more options to assist you should something unexpected occur.



From The Church To The Barn

To get to the Skinner Barn from the Church continue south on Main Street, then take a right on VT-100 South. Once you get into Waitsfield cross the Mad River on Medow Road and go South on N Road until you come to an intersection with Common Road. This will take you past the Von Trapp Greenhouse and Farm. Another 1000ft and you should find the big red Skinner Barn on your right. Parking is on the left hand side next to the house opposite the barn. You can review the directions on Google Maps by clicking here.

Local Taxi Cab Services

Please note taxi services do not operate as fluidly as in metropolitan areas. If you are planning to book a cab its best to do so the day before or be prepared to expect quite a long wait. The reception at the Barn will be ending at 10pm. We have not personally vetted these taxi services.
