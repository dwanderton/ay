Our story

Meeting at the Ocean Beach Fire Pits

We first met in San Francisco, CA when Alice was working for an organisation protecting the rights of low-income families facing aggressive eviction tactics during her second year of law school and when I (David) was consulting at a start-up, AirPair, providing video micro consultancy services.

Our first date started at Ocean Beach, and we held hands crossing the road from the Beach Chalet to the Fire Pits. I had invited Alice to come to a friend’s birthday party - a bonfire party on the beach! This also happened to be my first ever bonfire in the USA! We had an instant connection, and it was love at first sight - we shared our first kiss on the beach!

Maintaining and developing a relationship long distance for over two years, to say the least is not easy. But, we’ve been fortunate and blessed to have been able to see each other frequently and visit countries together, such as Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Iceland, Nicaragua, England, Scotland, Austria, alongside visiting various cities in the USA during our many road trips and 7-day trek from Boston to LA and back. Yes, Boston to LA and back in 7 days. Check out some of our photos from these adventures by clicking here.

We look forward to a life-long world of these adventures while sharing and enjoying these experiences with you. We strive to be action-oriented and dynamic, taking active choices over passivity. We are very excited to start the next chapter of our lives together and want to extend our warm and heartfelt thanks to you once again. This thanks is for your support, kindness, thoughtfulness, provision, opening of opportunity, mindfulness, dedication, and unwaivering faith in us. Thank you for being a part of our lives.

We are truly blessed by all of you, both those present today and those no longer with us.
